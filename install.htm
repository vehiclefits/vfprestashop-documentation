Instructions:
  1. Unzip the module to where PrestaShop is installed.
  2. Load up /vfadmin/public in your browser. Log in with your PrestaShop credentials. Go to settings, and enter "year,make,model" for the schema. Press GO to have the database created.
  3. In your PrestaShop admin, goto "modules" and find "vaf". Press install.
Precautions:
  • DISABLE your PrestaShop cache/compilation before installing any new modules.
  • BACKUP your store before installing any module
Notes:
  • Module hooks into "left column" hook. If your custom theme doesn't have this hook, it might not show up.
